The SWISH chat services allows you to chat with users that are looking at the same file as you are. SWISH displays an avatar for each user with whom you share an open file. This group of users defines your dynamic chat room.

The chat facility runs on top of the web socket interface. Currently, chat messages are simply broadcasted. They are not remembered by the server.

Status of the SWISH sharing service

What you are seeing are the first steps to allow users of SWISH to communicate. There are many facilities we have in mind, such as:

  • Share queries
  • Connect editors for collaborative editing
  • Make teams that connect people also if they are not editing the same file.
  • Allow Prolog to exchange messages through the chat room.
